# Company Profile



## Company name
VortexGear

## What we do
We are a direct-to-consumer gaming peripherals brand for competitive PC and esports players.

## Products
Mechanical keyboards, lightweight gaming mice, wireless headsets, and mousepads sold through our online store and Amazon.

## Market
Global PC gaming peripherals / esports gear.

## Target customer
Competitive gamers aged 16–30 who care about performance, aesthetics, and value — especially FPS and battle royale players.

## Positioning
Pro-level performance at a mid-premium price — faster than budget brands, more accessible than ultra-premium giants.

## Our differentiators
- Low-latency wireless tech tuned for competitive play
- Modular, customizable designs with RGB and swappable parts
- Strong community on Twitch, Discord, and TikTok
- Faster product iteration than legacy hardware brands

## Competitors we already watch
- Razer
- Logitech G
- SteelSeries
- Corsair
- HyperX

## Business goals
Grow D2C sales, expand into LATAM and Southeast Asia, and become the go-to brand for aspiring esports players.
